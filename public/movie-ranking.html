The Best Movies According to Jordan

My top movies of 2023

(In no particular order)

Oppenheimer

This movie provided further education on a major world event. This film contained an exceptional story, fantastic cast, and "explosive" scenes.

Society of the Snow

This film told a tragic story of a South American rugby team whose plan crashed in the Andes Mountains. The narration is of the journal entries from the men on the trip.
